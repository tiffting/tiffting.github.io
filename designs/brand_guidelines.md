# Personal Brand Guidelines
## Tiff Ting - Technical Product Translator

---

## Brand Identity

**Value Proposition:** Methodical problem-solver who translates complex technical requirements into user-centered solutions for mission-driven organizations

**Personality Traits:** Empathetic • Curious • Methodical • Open-minded • Humble

**Positioning:** Domain-agnostic problem solver with cross-industry pattern recognition, bridging technical complexity with human-centered outcomes

---

## Visual Identity

### Logo
- **Primary Mark:** Tangram bascule bridge (asymmetric, dynamic)
- **Symbol Meaning:** Bridge = connection/translation, Tangram = methodical problem-solving, Bascule = adaptive solutions
- **Usage:** Logo + text for primary applications, symbol-only for small sizes

### Color Palette

**Primary Colors:**
- **Trust Blue:** #2E5BBA (reliability, technical expertise)
- **Growth Green:** #4A9B8E (balance, sustainable solutions) 
- **Creative Purple:** #7B5BA6 (innovation, cross-domain thinking)
- **Golden Yellow:** #F2B705 (optimism, creative energy)

**Supporting Color:**
- **Light Neutral:** #E8F4F8 (backgrounds, subtle accents)

**Color Usage:**
- Blue: Primary brand color, headers, CTAs
- Green: Success states, growth metrics
- Purple: Innovation highlights, technical depth
- Yellow: Creativity accents, energy moments
- Neutral: Backgrounds, subtle elements

### Typography Recommendations
- **Headlines:** Inter, Poppins, or system fonts (clean, modern)
- **Body Text:** Source Sans Pro, system-ui (readable, professional)
- **Code/Technical:** JetBrains Mono, Consolas (when needed)

---

## Voice & Tone

### Communication Style
- **Balance:** Accessible yet expert-level
- **Tone:** Professional-warm over analytical-cold
- **Approach:** Lead with curiosity, focus on problem heart

### Key Messaging Themes
1. **Genuine curiosity** in finding the heart of problems
2. **Balanced solutions** considering time, budget, business goals, user needs
3. **Cross-industry pattern recognition** and creative connections
4. **Mission-driven** work and values alignment

### Writing Guidelines
- Start with the problem or outcome
- Use concrete examples over abstract concepts
- Acknowledge complexity while remaining accessible
- Show empathy for user/stakeholder perspectives

## Applications

### Business Cards
- Logo + name + title
- Clean, minimalist design
- QR code to portfolio (optional)

### Email Signature
- Name, title, website
- Small logo symbol
- Trust blue for links

### Portfolio Layout
- Case study structure: Challenge → Process → Solution → Impact
- Visual hierarchy with brand colors
- Clean typography, generous white space

### Social Media
- Consistent logo/colors across platforms
- Professional headshot in brand colors
- Bio emphasizing "Technical Product Translator"

## Brand Differentiators

### Unique Positioning
- Healthcare + Arts + SaaS experience combination
- Technical depth with empathetic user focus
- Mission-driven organization expertise
- Cross-industry pattern recognition

### Avoid These Perceptions
- Pure technical specialist (emphasize user focus)
- Single-industry expert (highlight versatility)
- Process-only consultant (show tangible outcomes)
